Our Mission

True Honest Academics (T.H.A.) promotes positive education and youth empowerment through our C.R.O.P.S. framework.

C.R.O.P.S. Framework

  • C – Conflict-Resolution: Employing the principles of freedom, wisdom, and victory to live a culture and way of righteousness, instilling respect, honor, and integrity.
  • R – Resilience & Optimism: Being a great person on your own cognition to be the best version of yourself, being what you want to see in the world.
  • O – Opportunity & Potential: Networking potential through problem solving and development of interpersonal skills through growth, sharing, empathy, and sharing self care.
  • P – Proper Socialization: A beautiful village of mastery of all of the above core goals that we strive to teach these babies and these youth.
  • S – Strategic Success: Always improvise, adapt, take another look for another approach until we overcome our 👨🏿‍🏫 problems!!!
